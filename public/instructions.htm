Instructions for HawkDove

Please read them carefully

To play this game you must make 3 types of decisions: 1.On the partner screen, you must first choose a partner to interact with. 2.Then you will be taken to the strategy choice screen. On the left side of the strategy screen, you must choose a strategy to play when you interact with that partner. 3.Last, other players may interact with you. These interactions will show up on the right hand side of the strategy screen. You must choose strategies to play when they interact with you. Every choice you make is timed. You will be given 3 seconds to choose a partner, and 3 seconds for every strategy choice.

This game moves quickly, please give your undivided attention for the duration of the 20 rounds. Missing a choice within the allotted time will carry a heavy penalty that negatively affects your earnings.

If you understood the instructions correctly press the "DONE" button to proceed to the game.
