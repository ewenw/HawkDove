Game Instructions


Please read them carefully

  • On the screen with many circles indicating your partners, choose one partner to visit.
  • Then choose an action to visit that partner with.
  • Other partners may nor may not visit you. For each visit, you have limited time to respond with an action.

  • Every choice you make is timed. This game moves quickly, please give your undivided attention for the duration of the 20 rounds. Missing a choice within the allotted time will carry a heavy penalty that negatively affects your earnings.
