Game Instructions


Please read them carefully

  1. 1. On the screen circles indicating the other players, choose one of them to visit.

  2. 2. Then choose an action to visit that player with.

  3. 3. Other players may nor may not visit you. If they do, you have limited time to respond back with an action.


    4. Every choice you make is timed. This game moves quickly, so please give your undivided attention for the duration of the 20 rounds. Missing a choice within the allotted time will carry a heavy penalty that reduces your earnings.
