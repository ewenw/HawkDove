Please read the following instructions carefully


  1. 1. On the screen where you can see circles indicating the other players, choose one of them to visit.

  2. 2. Choose an action to visit that player with.

  3. 3. Some other players may visit you.


    4. Every choice you make is timed. This game moves quickly, so please give your undivided attention for the duration of the 20 rounds. Failing to make a choice within the allotted time will carry a heavy penalty that reduces your earnings.
